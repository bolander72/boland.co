My recent roles have been as a startup founder, software developer, and independent consultant. Before working independently, I created and sold an agtech startup, worked at both new startups and Fortune 500 companies, and served as a UX designer and researcher. Prior to that, I did a stint as an entrepreneur in residence with Swiss EP.

I studied psychology and design in graduate school at the University of Brighton, UK. During that time, I worked as a researcher at design agencies and later partnered with The New Digital School in Porto, Portugal as a researcher in residence.

I enjoy working with both early-stage startups and larger companies across industries. I also mentor designers and developers at [ADPList](https://adplist.org/members/michael-boland).
